About Me

I'm currently an Assistant Researcher in the Smart Internet Group at Pengcheng National Laboratory, and I'm a recipient of the 2025 National Youth Talent Program. I received my Ph.D. degree from the School of Computer Science and Technology, East China Normal University, supervised by Prof. Edwin Hsing-Mean Sha (Changjiang Scholar, NSFC Distinguished Young Scholar).

Recently, my research interests include smart internet, embedded AI, LLM and hardware-software co-design. Specifically, my research focus on AI model training and inference acceleration for smart internet, AI model compression, hardware-software co-design for Embedded AI systems, and network for LLM inference, etc. So far, I have published over 20 papers at the international conferences/journals.
