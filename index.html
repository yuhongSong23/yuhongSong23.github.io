About Me

I'm currently an Assistant Researcher in the Smart Internet Group (SIG) at Peng Cheng National Laboratory (PCNL). Before joining PCNL in Sep. 2025, I was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at George Mason University (GMU) from Sep. 2024 to Aug. 2025, supervised by Prof. Weiwen Jiang. From Mar. 2024 to Aug. 2024, I also served as a Research Assistant in Prof. Zili Shao's group at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). I earned my Ph.D. degree from East China Normal University (ECNU) in Jun. 2024, under the supervision of Prof. Edwin Hsing-Mean Sha (National Thousand Talents Scholar, Changjiang Scholar, NSFC Distinguished Young Scholar).

My research interests include smart internet, embedded AI, and hardware-software co-design. Specifically, my research focus on AI model training and inference acceleration for smart internet, AI model compression, hardware-software co-design for Embedded AI systems, etc. So far, I have published over 20 papers at the international conferences/journals such as DAC, TCAD, ICCAD, ICCD, DATE, ASP-DAC, GLSVLSI, etc.
