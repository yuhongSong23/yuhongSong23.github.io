Bibliography

I'm a postdoctoral research fellow in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at George Mason University (GMU), supervised by Prof. Weiwen Jiang. Before I joined GMU in September 2024, I served as a research assistant in Prof. Zili Shao's group at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), from March to August 2024. I earned my Ph.D. degree from East China Normal University (ECNU) in June 2024, under the supervision of Prof. Edwin Hsing-Mean Sha (National Thousand Talents Scholar, Changjiang Scholar, NSFC Distinguished Young Scholar).

My research interests include quantum computing, quantum error correction, AI accelerator and hardware-software co-design. Specially, my research focuse on error correction and fault tolerance for Noisy Intermediate-Scale Quantum (NISQ) computers, optimization eechniques for quantum circuit simulation, and hardware-software co-design for AI systems, etc. So far, I have published 20 papers at the international conferences/journals such as DAC, ICCAD, TCAD, ICCD, DATE, ASP-DAC, GLSVLSI, etc.
